Tap Tempo

Qrest can help you find the tempo of a tune.

All you need to do is click the "TAP" button in sync with your tune's beats, and Qrest will give you the BPM and calculate new vaules in realtime.

On the very first clicks you'll notice a red light showing besides the "TAP" button. This is the steadiness indicator. It will turn green when Qrest detects that you are tapping steadily.

How is BPM calculated ?
Qrest keeps the last 4 clicks in memory and calculates the average of the gaps between them in order to get BPM
How does Qrest know I'm steady ?
Your are considered steady when the shortest gap is not shorter than 90% of the longest one
