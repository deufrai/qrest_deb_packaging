Tap Tempo

Qrest can help you in finding the tempo of a tune.

All you need to do is click the "TAP" button in sync with your tune's beats, and Qrest will give you the BPM and compute new vaules in realtime.

As you keep clicking the "TAP" button, the steadiness pie indicator will reflect how steadily you are clicking. The more green you see, the steadier you are. You can get a numerical value of your steadiness by hovering the pie. Let the tootlip reveal the natural drummer that sleeps inside of you.

Qrest computes the BPM by averaging the ellapsed time between your 4 last clicks
